FAQs

Get clarity on how Hashrid works, what you earn, and why this XP-powered protocol is the next step in Web3 social evolution. We’re building for creators, culture drivers, and crypto-native communities.

Do I need crypto to use Hashrid? +
No. You can join with just a wallet and start earning XP by posting, roasting, and participating. No tokens or crypto purchases are required to use core features.
What makes Hashrid different from X, Reddit or Discord? +
Hashrid is a Web3-native platform designed for community-driven engagement. Unlike X, Reddit, or Discord, it rewards users with XP for their contributions and offers on-chain features like NFT badges, gated experiences, and monetization based on activity.
What are NFT badges for? +
NFT badges represent a user’s achievements and milestones. They’re more than collectibles — they can unlock exclusive access to voice portals, events, or community features, and showcase your journey on-chain.
How is XP earned? +
XP is earned through multiple actions: posting content, receiving likes or replies, participating in Voice Portals, completing missions or bounties, and being active in the community. The more value you add, the more XP you gain.
Is there a token involved? +
There is no native token required to use the platform at this stage. However, in the future, a token may be introduced to enhance utility, reward loyal users, and support the economy around creators and communities.
What are XP-Gated Voice Portals? +
Voice Portals are live audio rooms that require a certain XP level to enter. This ensures quality interactions by rewarding active members with access to meaningful discussions, community calls, or exclusive sessions.
Can I really earn money from memes? +
Yes. Creators who generate high-engagement memes and posts can earn XP, gain visibility, and get selected for rewards, NFT drops, or bounties. Hashrid is designed to empower creators through decentralized rewards.
Can I use Hashrid without connecting my wallet? +
You can browse content anonymously, but to fully experience the platform — like posting, earning XP, and accessing features — you’ll need to connect a crypto wallet. It’s simple and takes just a few seconds.
